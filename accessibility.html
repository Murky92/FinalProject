Accessibility Commitment

We are committed to ensuring Tabletop Reserve is accessible to all users, regardless of abilities or disabilities

Our Commitment to Accessibility

At Tabletop Reserve, we believe that tabletop gaming should be accessible to everyone. We are committed to ensuring that our website and mobile application are accessible to people of all abilities, including those with disabilities.

We strive to meet or exceed the requirements of the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards. Our goal is to create an inclusive experience that allows all users to navigate, understand, and interact with our services effectively.

Accessibility Features

Tabletop Reserve incorporates the following accessibility features to improve usability for all users:

Website Accessibility

  • Semantic HTML structure with proper heading hierarchy
  • Descriptive alternative text for all images
  • Color contrast that meets WCAG 2.1 AA standards
  • Keyboard accessibility for all interactive elements
  • ARIA labels and landmarks to improve screen reader compatibility
  • Resizable text without loss of functionality
  • Focus indicators for keyboard navigation

Mobile App Accessibility

  • Support for screen readers (VoiceOver on iOS, TalkBack on Android)
  • Dynamic text sizing compatibility
  • Voice control support
  • Sufficient touch target sizes
  • Label and instructions for interactive elements
  • Ability to navigate without relying on color cues

Shop Information for Physical Accessibility

We also encourage our partner shops to provide detailed accessibility information in their profiles, including:

  • Wheelchair accessibility
  • Accessible parking availability
  • Accessible restroom facilities
  • Table height and space information
  • Lighting conditions
  • Quiet spaces or sensory considerations

Accessibility Standards

Our accessibility efforts are guided by established standards and best practices:

WCAG 2.1

We aim to conform to Web Content Accessibility Guidelines 2.1 Level AA standards across our digital platforms.

ADA Compliance

We work to ensure compliance with the Americans with Disabilities Act for our US users.

Section 508

Our platforms are designed with Section 508 requirements in mind for government accessibility standards.

Mobile Accessibility

We follow mobile accessibility best practices for iOS and Android platforms.

Using Assistive Technologies

Tabletop Reserve is designed to work with a variety of assistive technologies:

Screen Readers

Our website is compatible with popular screen readers including JAWS, NVDA, VoiceOver, and TalkBack. We use semantic HTML and ARIA attributes to ensure screen readers can properly interpret our content.

Keyboard Navigation

All features of our website can be accessed using keyboard-only navigation. Users can use the Tab key to navigate through interactive elements, Enter to activate links and buttons, and appropriate keystrokes for other controls.

Voice Recognition

Our website supports voice recognition software like Dragon NaturallySpeaking, allowing users to navigate and interact with voice commands.

Display Adjustments

Users can adjust text size using browser zoom functions without breaking the layout or functionality of our website. We maintain appropriate color contrast for users with low vision.

Known Limitations

While we strive for full accessibility, we acknowledge the following current limitations:

  • Some third-party content or embedded maps may not fully meet our accessibility standards
  • PDF documents may have varying levels of accessibility
  • Legacy content may not have been fully updated to current standards

We are actively working to address these limitations and improve our overall accessibility.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Tabletop Reserve

General Questions

What is Tabletop Reserve?

+

Tabletop Reserve is a platform that connects tabletop gaming enthusiasts with local gaming shops. Shop owners can list their tables and special events, while players can discover and book gaming spaces near them.

How does Tabletop Reserve work?

+

Shop owners register their business and list available tables and events. Players can use our mobile app to discover shops near them, view available tables, and make reservations. The platform handles the booking process, notifications, and helps shops manage their gaming spaces.

Is Tabletop Reserve available in my area?

+

Tabletop Reserve is currently available throughout the UK. We're expanding to new locations regularly. The best way to check is to download our app and search for shops in your area.

Do I need an account to use Tabletop Reserve?

+

Yes, both shop owners and players need to create a free account to use Tabletop Reserve. Shop owners register through this website, while players can create an account through our mobile app.

How do I get in touch with Tabletop Reserve support?

+

You can reach our support team through the Contact page, by email at support@tabletopreserve.com, or through the in-app chat support if you're using the mobile app.

For Shop Owners

How do I register my shop on Tabletop Reserve?

+

To register your shop, click on the "Register Shop" button in the top navigation. You'll need to provide basic information about your shop, including contact details and location. After submission, our team will review your application, and you'll receive an email confirmation once approved.

How much does it cost to list my shop?

+

Tabletop Reserve operates on a simple fee structure. Basic shop listing is free, and we charge a small commission (5%) on each reservation made through the platform. We also offer premium plans with additional features and reduced commission rates. Check our Pricing page for full details.

How do I set up my tables and events?

+

Once your shop is approved, you can log in to your dashboard where you'll find options to add tables and create events. For tables, you can specify details like table size, game types, and availability. For events, you can set dates, times, attendance limits, and other details. Our Shop Owner Guide provides step-by-step instructions.

How do refunds and cancellations work?

+

You can set your own cancellation policy in your shop settings (e.g., 24-hour notice required). When a customer cancels within your policy timeframe, the refund is processed automatically. For cancellations outside the policy timeframe, you can approve or deny them manually. Shop owners can also cancel reservations when necessary, with automatic refunds issued to customers.

How do I get paid for bookings?

+

Payments are processed through our secure payment system. You'll need to add your bank account details in the Payment section of your shop dashboard. We transfer funds to your account every week for all completed reservations, minus our service fee. You can view detailed payment reports in your dashboard.

For Players

How do I find gaming shops near me?

+

Download the Tabletop Reserve app from the App Store or Google Play Store. After creating an account, the app will show gaming shops near your location. You can filter results by distance, game types, amenities, and availability.

How do I make a table reservation?

+

In the app, browse available shops and tables. When you find a table you want to book, select your preferred date and time, specify the number of players, and complete the booking process. You'll receive a confirmation email and notification once your reservation is confirmed by the shop.

Can I cancel or modify my reservation?

+

Yes, you can cancel or modify your reservation through the app. Go to your bookings and select the reservation you want to change. Note that cancellation policies vary by shop, and cancellations made too close to the reservation time may not be eligible for a full refund.

How do I join an event at a gaming shop?

+

You can browse upcoming events in the Events section of the app. When you find an event you'd like to join, tap the Register button and follow the instructions. Some events are free, while others may have a registration fee. You'll receive event details and reminders via the app.

How do I leave a review for a shop?

+

After your reservation is complete, you'll receive a notification asking for your feedback. You can rate your experience and leave a review directly in the app. Your honest feedback helps other players and helps shops improve their services.

Account & Billing

How do I recover my password?

+

On the login page, click the "Forgot Password" link. Enter your email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. Click the link in the email and follow the instructions to create a new password.

How do I update my account information?

+

Log in to your account and go to your profile settings. Here, you can update your personal information, contact details, and notification preferences. For shop owners, you can also update your shop details, hours, and payment information in the Shop Settings section.

What payment methods are accepted?

+

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), as well as Apple Pay and Google Pay within the mobile app. Some shops may also offer pay-at-shop options for certain bookings.

Is my payment information secure?

+

Yes, all payment information is securely processed through our payment provider, which uses industry-standard encryption and security measures. We never store your full credit card details on our servers. Our platform is fully compliant with PCI DSS requirements.

How do I get a receipt for my booking?

+

After each successful booking, a receipt is automatically sent to your registered email address. You can also view and download receipts for all your bookings in the Bookings section of your account.

Technical Questions

What devices does the Tabletop Reserve app support?

+

Our mobile app is available for iOS devices running iOS 12 or later, and Android devices running Android 8.0 or later. The web dashboard for shop owners works on all modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge).

The app is crashing on my device. What should I do?

+

First, make sure your app is updated to the latest version. If problems persist, try these steps: 1) Restart the app, 2) Restart your device, 3) Uninstall and reinstall the app. If you're still experiencing issues, please contact our support team with details about your device model and operating system.

Does the app work offline?

+

The app requires an internet connection for most features, including searching for shops, making reservations, and receiving notifications. However, you can view your confirmed bookings offline if you've previously loaded them while connected.

How do I enable notifications?

+

When you first install the app, you'll be asked to allow notifications. If you declined initially, you can enable them in your device settings: go to Settings > Apps > Tabletop Reserve > Notifications. In the app, you can customize which types of notifications you receive in your Profile Settings.

What permissions does the app require?

+

The app requires the following permissions: Location (to find nearby shops), Camera (optional, for scanning QR codes), Notifications (for booking alerts), and Storage (to cache data for offline use). You can manage these permissions in your device settings.

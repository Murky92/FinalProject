Request Data Deletion

We value your privacy. Use this form to request the deletion of your personal data from Tabletop Reserve.

Important Information

When you request data deletion, we will permanently remove all your personal information from our systems, including:

  • Your account profile
  • Reservation history
  • Event bookings
  • Payment information (if any)
  • Communication preferences

Note: This action cannot be undone. Once your data is deleted, you'll need to create a new account if you wish to use Tabletop Reserve in the future.

This must match the email address used for your Tabletop Reserve account.

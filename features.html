Tabletop Reserve Features

Powerful tools to help shops manage their gaming spaces and connect with players

Easy Table Management

Manage your gaming tables with our intuitive dashboard. Define table types, capacities, and special features to help players find exactly what they're looking for.

  • Set up multiple table types (standard, premium, private rooms)
  • Define special features (terrain, power outlets, etc.)
  • Set table availability hours and blackout periods
  • Track table usage statistics and optimize your space

Event Management

Create and promote gaming events to attract players to your shop. Our event management system handles registrations, reminders, and even waitlists.

  • Create one-time or recurring events
  • Set attendance limits and registration requirements
  • Collect event RSVPs and manage check-ins
  • Send automated reminders to registered players

Customer Communication

Stay connected with your customers through our integrated notification system. Send updates about new events, promotions, or last-minute table availability.

  • Send targeted push notifications to app users
  • Create custom promotions and special offers
  • Automated reservation confirmations and reminders
  • Gather customer feedback after visits

Business Analytics

Make data-driven decisions with our comprehensive analytics dashboard. Track table bookings, monitor busy periods, and identify opportunities for growth.

  • Visual reports of booking trends and patterns
  • Customer retention and return visit metrics
  • Event attendance and registration analytics
  • Export data for further analysis

Discover Gaming Spaces

Find the perfect gaming venue near you with our intuitive search and filter system. Browse shops based on location, table types, games, and amenities.

  • Location-based search with map integration
  • Filter by table types and special features
  • View shop profiles with photos and details
  • Read reviews from other players

Seamless Reservations

Book gaming tables with just a few taps. Our mobile app makes it easy to find available tables, reserve your spot, and manage your bookings.

  • Real-time availability calendar
  • Quick booking process with saved preferences
  • Manage, modify, or cancel reservations
  • Reservation confirmation and reminders

Join Gaming Events

Never miss a game night again. Discover and register for events at your favorite shops, from tournaments to paint nights to new game demonstrations.

  • Browse upcoming events in your area
  • RSVP and secure your spot
  • Get notified about new events matching your interests
  • Connect with other attendees

Player Community

Connect with fellow gamers who share your interests. Our community features help you find gaming groups, make new friends, and get more game sessions on your calendar.

  • Create a player profile with game preferences
  • Find players with similar interests
  • Join game-specific communities
  • Share photos and experiences from your sessions

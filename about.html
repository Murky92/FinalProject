About Tabletop Reserve

Connecting tabletop gaming enthusiasts with local shops and events since 2023

Our Mission

Tabletop Reserve was created with a simple mission: to make tabletop gaming more accessible and help local gaming shops thrive in an increasingly digital world.

We believe that tabletop gaming creates unique social experiences and builds communities in ways that digital gaming cannot replicate. Our platform helps players discover local shops, reserve tables, and join events - ensuring that the tabletop gaming tradition continues to flourish.

Who We Are

We're a team of passionate tabletop gamers who understand both the joy of playing and the challenges of running a gaming business. Our diverse team includes:

  • Experienced tabletop gamers across multiple genres
  • Former and current game shop owners
  • Software developers with a passion for gaming
  • Community organizers from various gaming scenes

This combination of perspectives allows us to create a platform that truly serves the needs of both players and shop owners.

How It Works

1

For Shop Owners

Register your gaming space, set up your tables, and manage your calendar through our simple dashboard. Our platform helps you fill empty tables and attract new customers.

2

For Gamers

Download our mobile app to discover nearby gaming shops, browse available tables, and make reservations. You can also find and register for local events all in one place.

3

Creating Community

Both players and shops can build profiles, earn reputation, and connect with the local gaming community. Our optional matchmaking features even help you find fellow players.
